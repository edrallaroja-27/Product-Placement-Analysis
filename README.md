## 1.Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the impact of product placement on sales performance using Tableau. The analysis examines how factors such as product category, product position, pricing, promotions, seasonal trends, consumer demographics, and foot traffic influence sales volume.

Using interactive visualizations, KPI cards, dashboards, and Tableau Stories, the project transforms raw retail data into meaningful business insights. The results help businesses understand customer behavior, evaluate product performance, optimize product placement, and support data-driven decision-making for improved sales and marketing strategies.



## 2. Objectives

- Analyze how product positioning influences sales performance.
- Examine the impact of pricing strategies on sales volume.
- Evaluate the effectiveness of promotional activities across product categories.
- Identify seasonal trends affecting product sales.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior using consumer demographics.
- Study the relationship between foot traffic and sales volume.
- Compare product prices with competitor prices to understand pricing competitiveness.
- Develop interactive visualizations, dashboards, and stories using Tableau.
- Generate actionable business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

##  Dataset

The dataset used for this project is **Product Positioning.csv**, which contains information related to product placement, pricing, promotions, customer demographics, and sales performance.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Product Category | Category of the product |
| Product Position | Product placement location |
| Season | Sales season |
| Price | Selling price |
| Competitor Price | Competitor's selling price |
| Sales Volume | Number of units sold |
| Promotion | Promotion status |
| Consumer Demographics | Customer segment |
| Foot Traffic | Customer footfall |

> 📁 Dataset used: **https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/amitvkulkarni/impact-of-product-positioning-on-sales** (Tableau built-in) + supplementary placement data

## 🚀 How to Use

### Prerequisites
- [Tableau Desktop](https://www.tableau.com/products/desktop) or [Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/) (free)

## Steps


1. Download the project files from this repository.
2. Open the Tableau workbook (`.twbx`) using Tableau Public or Tableau Desktop.
3. Review the worksheets to understand each visualization.
4. Open the interactive dashboard to explore KPIs, charts, and filters.
5. View the Tableau Story to understand the complete business analysis.
6. Apply the available filters to analyze different product categories, product positions, seasons, and promotions.
